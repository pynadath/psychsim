# Research method category #

Survey


# Specific question #

* What is your age?
* How many children do you have?
* Do you have pets?
* Do you have a full-time job?
* When you attempted to enter the shelter, were you allowed entry? (Response format: region, yes / no)
* What was your level of wealth at the end of the previous tax season? Provide your answer on a scale of 1 to 7, where 1 is the lowest level and 7 is the highest.
* What was your income at the end of the previous tax season? Provide your answer on a scale of 1 to 7, where 1 is the lowest level and 7 is the highest.
* What is your injury level? Provide your answer on a scale of 1 to 7, where 1 is the lowest level and 7 is the highest.
* What is your anticipated risk of evacuation? Provide your answer on a scale of 1 to 7, where 1 is the lowest level and 7 is the highest.
* What is your level of dissatisfaction with the government’s response during the previous hurricane? If there was no previous hurricane in your region during the time period under consideration, respond with N/A. Otherwise, provide your answer on a scale of 1 to 7, where 1 is the lowest level and 7 is the highest.




# Sampling strategy #

Sample 20% of the individuals who attempt to enter each shelter in each region, over all of the timesteps in the IDP.
